## Stroller Buying Guide

**Full-size vs. lightweight:** Full-size strollers offer more features and comfort; lightweight/compact models prioritize portability. Many families end up owning both.

**Travel systems:** Infant car seat + stroller combos are convenient for newborns but the car seat outgrows quickly (typically 30-35 lbs).

**Key specs to check:**
- Weight (you'll lift this in/out of cars constantly)
- Fold complexity (one-hand vs. two-hand)
- Basket size (you need more storage than you think)
- Canopy coverage (full coverage matters in sun and rain)
- Wheel type (foam/rubber for urban; pneumatic for rough terrain)
