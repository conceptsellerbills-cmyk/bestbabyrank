## Why Baby Wipes Matter More Than You Think

Most new parents underestimate how much impact baby wipes have on infant skin health. A newborn's skin is up to 30% thinner than adult skin, making it far more permeable and reactive to chemicals, fragrances, and preservatives. The wrong wipe can cause contact dermatitis, diaper rash flare-ups, or chronic dryness.

The best baby wipes for sensitive skin should be:
- **Fragrance-free** — synthetic scents are the #1 trigger for skin reactions in babies
- **Alcohol-free** — drying agents strip the natural moisture barrier
- **Hypoallergenic** — formulated to minimize the risk of allergic reactions
- **Soft and thick** — rough wipes can micro-abrade delicate skin
- **pH-balanced** — ideally close to a baby's natural skin pH of 5.5

Huggies Natural Care Sensitive ticks all of these boxes. Let's break down exactly why.
